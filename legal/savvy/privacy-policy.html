1. Introduction

Welcome to Savvy, developed by Devikon Inc. ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience when using our video downloading application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Summary: Savvy is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data necessary for the app to function and do not sell your personal information to third parties.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

When you use Savvy, you may provide us with:

  • URLs and Links: The video URLs you paste or share with the app for downloading content
  • Account Information: If you create an account, we collect your email address and display name (optional)
  • Feedback and Support: Any information you provide when contacting us for support

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use our App, we automatically collect certain information:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App usage statistics, features used, crash reports
  • Analytics Data: Anonymous usage patterns to improve app functionality

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT access your social media account credentials
  • We do NOT store the videos you download on our servers
  • We do NOT track your browsing history outside our app
  • We do NOT collect location data
  • We do NOT access your contacts, photos, or other personal files

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's core functionality
  • Process your video download requests
  • Improve and optimize our App's performance
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Send important notifications about the App (with your consent)
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

Downloaded videos are stored locally on your device in your chosen storage location. We do not have access to these files after they are downloaded.

4.2 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit using HTTPS/TLS
  • Secure storage of any account credentials using industry-standard encryption
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Limited access to personal data by authorized personnel only
